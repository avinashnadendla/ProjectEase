About Us

Management is very important to organizations and institutions, as allows them to keep everything under check to prevent accidents which may result in loss of important data. But, at the same time it is also a very cumbersome and tedious task. ProjectEase will provide

  • Statistical Data, Visual Representation like graphs, Code, and snapshots of project etc.
  • Students who are uncomfortable and find it hard to reach out to their respective faculty can now easily communicate with them through the chat box
  • A Feedback Section to recieve valuable reviews from faculty

Wasting crucial human resources on easy and repetitive tasks is not efficient for big institutions. In most team-based projects, Professors find it hard to see the progress of the Teams. They do not know what each member of the team is doing to provide value to the project. Another problem is Comparing projects.
